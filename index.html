ROADMAP

PHASE 1 - CHRONOFACE TEASER & GROWTH OF SOCIAL PLATFORMS

During pre-mint, community and brand building will be the main focus of the team. Watchlist creation will be made for engagaged members of the community

PHASE 2 - PRIVATE AND PUBLIC MINT

Whitelisted members will be able to mint first for 24 hours, subsequently Public mint will start. Mint Price and Date have yet to be determined

PHASE 3 - EXCLUSIVE PHYGITAL PERKS FOR HOLDERS ONLY

A Mobile App will be created exclusively for holders of Chronoface NFT.Watch faces can be integrated and applied to Apple and Android smartwatches

PHASE 4 - TREASURY CREATION

To move and expand as a Brand, part of the funds will be reinvested back into the project for future creation and development. This treasury will be beneficial to holders by incentivizing holder's benefits, future creation, and development of Chronoface.

PHASE 5 - CHRONO DAO

NFT Holders will be part of our very own decentralized autonomous organization (DAO), where holders can participate in future watch related events, collaborations and special access to a watch fund.

PHASE 6 - CHRONO FUND

NFT Holders can explore new alternative investments in a watch fund, where the fund will own a collection of real world luxury watches that will create unlimited value appreciation for NFT holders.A minimal capital can start your journey to owning a fractional part of the end.
